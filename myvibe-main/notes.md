To begin using the app, press the "Get Started" button on the home screen. Account Registration:

If you don’t have an account, tap on "Register Here" to create a new account. If you already have an account, simply click on the "Sign In Here" text located below the "Register Here" button. Login Process:

After selecting "Sign In Here", enter your registered email and password to log into your account. App Features:

Once logged in, you can create your own vibe by selecting the appropriate option from the home screen. You can also browse and view the vibes of your friends. To interact with your friends, the app allows you to send messages directly to them. Logging Out:

To log out of your account, go to the Home screen, tap the Settings icon, and then select "Logout".
